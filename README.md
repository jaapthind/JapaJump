## 🛠️ THE QUANTUM TECH TREE
*Unlock these via the in-game Shop to break the laws of physics.*

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **VOID PLATING** is your best friend. It turns a fatal fall into an emergency reboot, keeping your altitude locked.

* **📡 Quantum Radar:** Expands the FOV (Field of View) so you can plan jumps 200m ahead.
* **🧬 Carbon Scrubbers:** Passively generates Credits while you move through the atmosphere.
