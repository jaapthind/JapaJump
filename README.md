# 🚀 JAPA JUMP: DON'T LOOK DOWN
**Official SDG 13: Climate Action Edition**

A high-stakes, physics-based vertical climber where every meter counts. Navigate a procedurally generated world, manage your energy, and survive the "Devil Zone" to save the planet.

---

## 🎮 How to Play

### ⌨️ Desktop Controls
- **Move:** `WASD` or `Arrow Keys`
- **Jump:** `Space` or `W` (Triple-jump unlockable!)
- **Pause:** `ESC`

### 📱 Mobile Controls
- **Joystick:** Slide the 'MOVE' button to steer.
- **Jump:** Tap the 'JUMP' button.

---

## ✨ Engineering Masterclass

* **Zone-Crossing Checkpoints:** Guaranteed **Blue Platforms** and **Waving Flags (🚩)** every 500m. Our custom "Zone Math" ensures you never miss a save point.
* **Energy Management:** Your suit drains energy as you climb. Perform precise jumps to conserve power.
* **System Failure Quizzes:** If you hit 0% energy, initiate a "Reboot Quiz." Answer Climate Action questions correctly to restore 100% power and continue from your current position.
* **The Tech Shop:** Spend earned Credits on permanent upgrades like **Ion Thrusters**, **Solar Panels**, and **Void Plating**.
* **Global Telemetry:** Full Cloud-Sync integration. Save your highscore and upgrades across devices via our secure satellite network.

---

## 🔥 The Devil Zone (9,000m+)
At 9,000 meters, the atmosphere thins and the sky turns crimson. Platforms shrink to their minimum size. Only the most skilled pilots reach the **Golden Platform at 10,000m**.
